Market Analysis: UK

Student numbers at UK language schools increased by 10 per cent between 2023 and 2024 with agents becoming increasingly important. Schools report having to be inventive with new courses in order to stay competitive,.

Key points

  • 28 schools participated in the 2024 ST survey on the UK
  • 68,653 total number of students at these schools in 2024
  • 205,959 total number of student weeks at these schools in 2024
  • 10 per cent increase in total student numbers between 2023 and 2024
